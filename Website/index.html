Why Choose Ninja IoT?

One-Click Setup

Build your custom IoT control panel instantly. Whether it's a switch, a slider, or a sensor readout, our drag-and-drop one-click setup gets your dashboard ready in seconds—no frontend code required.

Logic & Events

Automate your entire project visually. Create powerful if-then rules, loops, and actions like sending emergency Emails or triggering other devices using our intuitive block-based logic interface. Perfect for educational learning and complex systems alike.

Automatic APIs

Read and write data programmatically from anywhere. Every project automatically generates structured API endpoints for writing values, fetching history, and seamlessly integrating with third-party web services.

About Us

We are RoboNinja. Ninja IoT is specially made for educational purposes, providing a frictionless entry into the Internet of Things. Born from the frustration of over-complicated cloud platforms, our goal is to provide the perfect balance between absolute simplicity and boundless capabilities.

Perfect for Makers and Engineering students, from custom ESP integrations to powerful analytics—we build the tools so you can build the future.
